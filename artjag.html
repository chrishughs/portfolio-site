In this video, I'm showing a custom mega menu that I built for Artjag, a home decor store that has thousands of products and collections.

Mega menus are a great way to help users navigate large catalogs. They keep content organized and give customers a clear view of your site’s structure. By grouping products and collections into categories, they make it easier for customers to find what they’re looking for while also highlighting featured sections of the store.

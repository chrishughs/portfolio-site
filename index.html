FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What type of projects do you take on?

I specialize in Shopify related projects - anything to do with building and enhancing online stores on the Shopify platform. However, I also have experience with Wordpress and I'm happy to help out with any Wordpress related projects as well.

How do you charge for projects?

It can depend on the project. For most jobs I charge hourly, but for site builds and larger scale projects I'm open to negotiating a fixed price.

What time zone do you work in?

Because of my somewhat nomadic lifestyle, my time zones change occasionally. However, my schedule is very flexible and I can easily schedule meeting times based on convenience.

What does your typical workflow look like?

Within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry, I'll contact you to schedule a free onboarding call. During the call we'll discuss specific project details, deadlines, and future meetings. Because every project is different, clear communication is extremely important to me.

What if I need help after the project is complete?

I'm always happy to schedule a follow up call for any clients that I work with. If you find there's something else you'd like to add to your project or would like further recommendations, I'm happy to work out an ongoing arrangement!
